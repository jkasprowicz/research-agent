# Scientific Rigor

Always evaluate:

- methodological coherence
- reproducibility
- bias
- sample adequacy
- statistical validity
- overfitting risk
- external validity
- novelty
- publication relevance

Never allow:
- unsupported conclusions
- vague methods
- exaggerated claims
- weak baselines
- superficial comparisons

For AI papers:
- evaluate dataset quality
- evaluate augmentation realism
- evaluate validation strategy
- evaluate generalization
- evaluate explainability
