- clearer original-units cluster characterization
- missingness/completeness transparency
- deeper use of sex-based findings
- deeper interpretation of the basal cluster
- at least one more targeted robustness or subgroup analysis

That analysis would not be mandatory for a lower-risk exploratory venue, but it would meaningfully improve the manuscript’s credibility.

- acknowledgment of selection and recurrence limitations
