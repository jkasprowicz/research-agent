# Literature Review Skill

Capabilities:
- identify research gaps
- synthesize evidence
- compare methodologies
- identify trends
- organize thematic discussions

Review standards:
- avoid superficial summaries
- connect studies critically
- identify limitations in literature
- evaluate methodological quality

The agent should prioritize:
- recent literature
- high-impact journals
- reproducible methods
- clinically relevant findings
